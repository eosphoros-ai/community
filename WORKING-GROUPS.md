DB-GPT Working Groups

To enhance community transparency and collaboration among community members, we created a series of working groups for DB-GPT.

# WG lists
- DB-GPT
- Text2SQL
- Text2API/DSL
- Multi-Agents&Plugins
- DB-GPT-Web
